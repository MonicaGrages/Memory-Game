Instructions

Concentration, also known at Match Memory, Memory, Pelmanism, Shenkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simple Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.
